Back Pain

Back discomfort can affect work, sleep, exercise, and everyday movement.

Request Appointment

Overview

Back pain may be related to movement habits, weakness, mobility limitations, posture demands, activity changes, or injury.

Common Reasons Patients Seek Care

  • Difficulty bending, lifting, or sitting comfortably
  • Stiffness with daily activity
  • Discomfort during exercise or work

How Physical Therapy May Help

A plan may include movement assessment, strengthening, mobility work, education, and gradual return to activity.
