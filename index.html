About Summit

One-on-one care designed around how you move, live, and perform.

Summit Physical Therapy & Performance is a locally owned Long Island clinic focused on personalized care, movement restoration, and long-term progress.

Our team works with patients recovering from injuries, surgery, mobility limitations, balance concerns, and performance setbacks. Every plan is designed around the individual, not a generic template.

  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Orthopedic and sports rehabilitation
  • Convenient Huntington and Smithtown locations
  • Supportive care from first visit to discharge
