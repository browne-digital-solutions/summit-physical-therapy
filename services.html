Service FAQ

Common questions about care

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit typically includes a conversation about your goals, a movement assessment, and an initial care plan.

How long are appointments?

Appointment length may vary by service and patient needs. Our team can explain scheduling details before your first visit.

Do I need a referral?

Referral requirements depend on your insurance plan and situation. Our office can help you understand what may be needed.

Can physical therapy help athletes?

Yes, our team works with athletes and active adults on rehab, strength, movement quality, and return-to-sport progression.

Do you accept insurance?

Summit works with many major insurance providers and can help verify benefits before your first appointment.

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to move and makes it easy to assess the area being treated.
