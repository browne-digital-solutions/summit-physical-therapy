Your First Visit

What to expect at your appointment

Your first appointment helps your therapist understand your history, movement, goals, and the best path forward.

1

History Review

Talk through your reason for visiting, activity level, symptoms, goals, and relevant background.

2

Movement Assessment

Your therapist may assess mobility, strength, balance, posture, or functional movement.

3

Goal Discussion

Discuss what matters most to you, whether that is daily function, sport, work, or active living.

4

Initial Plan

Your therapist will explain the general plan of care and next steps based on your evaluation.

5

Home Guidance

If appropriate, you may receive general exercises or education to support your progress.

Before Your Appointment

A few simple tips before you arrive

Preparing ahead of time can make your first appointment smoother and more productive.

⏰

Arrive Early

Plan to arrive a few minutes early so our team can complete check-in and answer questions.

👕

Dress For Movement

Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to move, stretch, walk, or exercise as appropriate.

🎯

Know Your Goals

Think about what you want to return to, such as work, exercise, sports, hobbies, or daily activity.

💬

Communicate Changes

Tell your therapist about changes in symptoms, medications, surgeries, or provider instructions.

📅

Plan Follow-Ups

Your therapist and scheduling team can help coordinate future visits based on your plan.

📞

Ask Questions

Our office team can help with scheduling, insurance, location details, and general questions.
