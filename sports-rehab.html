Athlete-Focused Evaluation

We look beyond the injury.

Sports rehabilitation starts with understanding how the athlete moves, trains, competes, and performs. The goal is to build a plan that fits the demands of the sport and the individual.

Evaluations may include strength testing, mobility assessment, movement screening, balance work, and discussion of training history and goals.

Return-To-Sport Process

A structured path back to activity

Every athlete progresses differently. Our process is designed to help guide the transition from rehab to performance with clear milestones and individualized progression.

1

Restore Mobility

Address movement limitations, comfort, and early activity tolerance.

2

Build Strength

Develop control, stability, and strength for sport-specific demands.

3

Reintroduce Movement

Gradually progress running, jumping, cutting, lifting, or throwing patterns.

4

Progress Confidence

Use structured drills and progressions to rebuild trust in movement.

5

Return Stronger

Support the transition back to sport, training, and long-term performance.
