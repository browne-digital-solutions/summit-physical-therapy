Billing FAQ

Common insurance questions

Do I need a referral?

Referral requirements depend on your insurance plan and individual situation. Our office can help explain what may be needed.

Will insurance cover physical therapy?

Coverage varies by plan. Benefits, copays, deductibles, and visit limits are different for each patient.

What if my plan has a copay?

If your plan includes a copay, that amount may be due at the time of service. Plan details should be confirmed with your insurer.

Can I use out-of-network benefits?

Some patients may have out-of-network coverage. Your insurance provider can confirm eligibility and reimbursement rules.

Do you offer self-pay visits?

Patients may contact the office to ask about direct payment options and availability.

Can you verify my benefits?

Our office can help review general benefit information, but verification is not a guarantee of payment or coverage.
